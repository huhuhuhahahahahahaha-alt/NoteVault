Codex Tenebris

The Thirteen of the Inkless Flame

I. Scriptorium – The Binder of Meaning

"He carved sense into sound."

Domain: Language and definition. Symbol: A serpent devouring its own tongue. He is the reason things mean something at all. Liars choke; poets burn.

II. Caligra – The Mother of Shadows

"She was the first silhouette — the memory of what light forgot."

Domain: Form and reflection. Every copy loses something of the original.

III. Vocira – The Whispering Ember

"She breathes between thoughts."

Domain: Inspiration and madness. Offers brilliance at the price of sleep.

IV. Thassun – The Mirror Without Glass

"He reflects what never existed."

Domain: False memory and dream logic. The reflection answers honestly, then forgets you.

V. Elgareth – The Feathered Judge

"Every sin weighs a word."

Domain: Judgment and balance. Those who speak unjustly lose the ability to write.

VI. Arkenveil – The Keeper of Quiet Flame

"She lights no room — only memory."

Domain: Death’s recollection. Those who call her see their ancestors for one breath.

VII. Scriptura – The Ink That Remembers

"Her name was never meant to be written twice."

Domain: Preservation and repetition. Every time her name is written, she remembers the author.

VIII. Mordain – The Contract of Flesh

"He binds what gods abandon."

Domain: Pacts and oaths. Those who swear falsely hear his claws scratching paper forever.

IX. Lethrae – The Dream Eater

"She walks the border between sleep and drowning."

Domain: Dream and illusion. All who wake forget her; she remembers all who sleep.

X. Othrin – The Mouthless Serpent

"He devoured his own voice to silence the stars."

Domain: Secrets and earthly silence. When he moves, earthquakes whisper forgotten prayers.

XI. Malpheor – The Weaver of Ends

"Every destiny is a thread, every death a knot."

Domain: Fate and entropy. He can weave endings but not beginnings.

XII. Viraen – The Crimson Voice

"He sings in the heartbeat before fear."

Domain: Emotion and instinct. Those who hear his hum gain courage once — and never again.

XIII. The Nameless One

"The Thirteenth was silence itself. Every story ends with it."

Domain: Closure. Where its name is spoken, ink dries, stories end, and memory dies.
